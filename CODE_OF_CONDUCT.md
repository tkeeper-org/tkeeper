# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We expect respectful and professional behavior in issues, PRs, and discussions.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical discussion and outcomes.
- Critique code/ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent, ask for clarification before escalating.
- If you make a mistake, acknowledge it and move on.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation when behavior crosses the line.

## Reporting
To report a CoC issue, email: labs@tkeeper.org
